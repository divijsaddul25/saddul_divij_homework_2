Story of Simon Riley


Lieutenant Simon "Ghost" Riley is one of the protagonist in Call Of Duty Modern Warfare 2. He is a British special forces operator.
He is seen wearing dark red sunglasses and a skull patterned balaclava along with headphones with a mic.
Ghost was born in Manchester and he had a terrible childhood because he had abusive parents and his brother would scare him with a mask at night.
When he grew up, he joined the British millitary and returned back home in 2003 when he saw his whole family ruined.
He decided to stay and fix everyone of them. He helped his brother to quit drug and he kicked his father out of the house.
When he re joined the special forces later on, he was betrayed by Shepherd and was killed.
